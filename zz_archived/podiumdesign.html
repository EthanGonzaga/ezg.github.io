Lendlease Podium, aims to modernise the design, construction and operation of buildings. It is designed to speed up creation of multiple building designs to improve performance, sustainability, occupant experiences and other outcomes. To achieve this, Lendlease has been connecting previously siloed data across the property value chain. The goal is to connect development, construction and operation dat onto a single user platform

The product portfolio has been designed to support the built world from the identification and development of land, the assembly and management of a building and the provision of multi utility services to owners and tenants. Lendlease Podium is the enabler of the autonomous building of tomorrow. Such Products as:

Podium For Development:

Is a generative design tool, which streamlines and improves decision making along the property development process, from feasibility through to design and construction. It can generate building designs with real time financial analysis and detailed bill of materials in minutes.

Podium Supply Automation:

Enables suppliers, developers, and project managers to have a clear line of communication, in real time. The platform allows Lendlease to design the construction materials to meet the requirements of world-leading suppliers
