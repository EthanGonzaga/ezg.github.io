We have a goal to create better products, more efficiently and with greater certainty. We believe this goal can only be achieved by the right combination of technology and people.

MX Studio is proposed as a physical space occupied by a mix of Lendlease people and specialists provided by our supply chain partners. It is a creative space for engineers and designers, a collaboration space to meet as a whole team and to showcase work to customers, a fabrication space to enable rapid prototyping and a validation space for the prototypes.

Our Contextual Universal Building System. Content creation requires collecting requirements, designing, prototyping, evaluating solutions and defining specifications. Once proven these parts are codified in the CUBS workflow. Only then is content ready for a release for use by our design automation software and for fabrication by the supply park or remote suppliers.
