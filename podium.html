Podium for Development is a generative design tool, which streamlines and improves decision making along the property development process, from feasibility through to design and construction. It can generate building designs with real time financial analysis and detailed bill of materials in minutes.

As a Product Designer with a background in architecture and computational design, I have played a key role in bringing Podium for Development to life. By conducting user research and testing to identify the needs and pain points of Lendlease SMEs, designing end-to-end features that are both user-friendly and efficient and collaborating with internal stakeholders to ensure that designs meet the needs of the business
